BACK - UP UTILITY

Close the main application, before taking back-up or the back-up may contain errors.
Back-Up Data

It is recommended that you always take back-up after using your application or atleast once everyday. Store your back-up at some safe location, like some other hard drive or CD. Remember, that your application can be installed again but your data cannot be restored, if you lose it in any way and don't have the back-up data. It has become much easier now a days to corrupt or lose data, in presence of various silent computer worms and viruses, as well as sudden power shut downs.

Restore Back-Up

This will replace your current data with the data in back-up. If you are having any problems with your current data then it is recommended that you restore your back-up but if the back-up is not up-to-date then you would not be able to restore data entered after you last took back-up of your data. It is always advised that you take back-up of your current data before restoring older one.
